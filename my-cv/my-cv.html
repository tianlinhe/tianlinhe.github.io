Research Experience

    8/2018-present         Iterative Shrinkage-Thresholding Algorithm (ISTA) Network+

  • Directed by Dr. Yunmei Chen, Distingushed Professor of Mathematics.

  • Replaced Lasso in non-convex ISTA-Net+ with Minimum Concave Penalty (MCP). Proposed Multi-Scale Sparsity and Momentum-like Reverse Connection.

  • Experiment showed PSNR increased about 0.5% compared to ISTA-Net+ in image reconstruction and compressive sensing. Paper is expected to submit to MIDL 2019, MICCAI.

    • 11/2017-11/2018         Single-Shot Detector for Modeling Bio-Medical Data

  • Solved the prior anchor boxes for Single-Shot MultiBox Detector under equal-proportion interval principle in deep convolution neural networks.

  • Designed Foveal Context-integrated Detection Module: cascade residual-inception module with local context information and deconv module with broader background information.

  • Directed by Dr. Toler-Franklin of Computer Science. The detection accuracy increased by 2.4% in indistinguishable tumors. Paper was submitted to CVPR 2019.

    • 7/2017-8/2017         The Application of Instance Segmentation in Autonomous Vehicles

  • Short-term summer research with Associate Dr. Xuefeng Zhu in Dalian University of Technology.

  • This research is aimed to investigate the application of simultaneous objects detection and segmentation in assisting self-piloting automobiles. The deep-learning scene parsing was achieved in Caffe, while the training set and the validation set are the real road scene images.

    • 2/2017-6/2017         Heterogeneous Collaborative CAD Framework Research and Implementation                                       of Auto Structure Parts

  • A heterogeneous collaborative platform for CAD was established. The platform is based on COMX (A simplified Component Object Model for X Platforms), and the message transmission is achieved by self-defined simplified NMC (Neural Modeling Command). Per the demand of users, personalized UI and functional module are implemented in UGNX and CATIA through secondary development.

  • We creatively put forward a conflict management strategy in heterogeneous collaborative design based on the deep learning.

  • The thesis was under the supervision of Dr. Xiangkui Zhang in Dalian University of Technology.

    3/2015-4/2016         Method of Improving the Efficiency of Solar Panel on Vehicle through                                        Lowering the Working Temperature

  • Research proposal was successfully approved for National Undergraduate Innovation Project.

  • Under the supervision of Dr. Wenbin Hou in Dalian University of Technology.

  • Aimed to investigate the factors affecting solar battery cooling effiency, and the method of improving the efficiency through forecd convection and transpiration. Considered this both under theoretical conditions and practical engineering.
